About Pangea English

Pangea English is a freelance English service that strives to make learning fun and effective through a combination of individually designed English programs and a fun learning environment.

The word Pangea refers to a time when all of the countries in the world were geographically closer to one another. By learning English with Pangea English, you will feel more connected to a closer-knit, global community of English speakers

Lessons take place at various locations around Frankfurt Am Main and can be for individuals or small groups. Skype lessons are also available.

If you are interesting in learning more, send us a message!

Individual, Effective, Fun Methods

Individual

Every lesson program is centered around the students learning habits and goals. One way a student can keep track of their progress is reviewing their own interactive Mind Map. After each lesson, the teacher will add to their current mind-map to allow students to build associations and remember others when the lesson is over.

To see an example of this, look at Wolfgang Thomman's Mind Map. Our imaginary student only cares about two things; coffee and cars. But he will learn all of the grammar structures and vocabulary he needs to succeeded in the English world through his interests!

Fun and humor not only makes learning more enjoyable, but it actually allows connections made in your brain to be stronger! By always communicating in English, you will learn organically through necessity by speaking about things that you feel passionate about
