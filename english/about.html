About English with Russell

English with Russell is a freelance English service that strives to make learning fun and effective through a combination of individually designed English programs and a fun and engaging learning environment. English with Russell does not stick to one method or another for teaching, but uses a combination of several based on an individuals most effective or preferred learning conditions

Lessons take place at various locations and can be for individuals or small groups. Skype lessons are also available!

If you are interesting in learning more, send me a message!

Individual, Effective, Fun Methods

Individual

Every lesson program is centered around the students learning habits and goals. One way a student can keep track of their progress is reviewing their own interactive Mind Map. After each lesson, the teacher will add to their current mind-map to allow students to build associations and remember others when the lesson is over.

To see an example of this, look at Wolfgang Thomman's Mind Map. Our imaginary student only cares about two things; coffee and cars. But he will learn all of the grammar structures and vocabulary he needs to succeeded in the English world through his interests!

Effective

THere are many methods of teaching English out there. I have read, studied and used many of them and can tell you that there is no universal way to learn a language. Everybody is different and the wrong method for that individual will lead to ineffective learning. I have found the best method to be a blended method of ways that work for the individual....you!

Some potential learners thing they are too old or too busy to learn a new language, but this just isn't true. The best time to learn a language is always now! Whether you are a quick learner or slow, we will work together to create a blend of time tested methods tailored around your schedule and learning style. Ready to begin? Contact me

Fun!

Fun and humor not only makes learning more enjoyable, but it actually allows connections made in your brain to be stronger! If you are not enjoying what you are studying, then you probably will not retain as much of it afterwards. This is the cornerstone of Pangea English. By always communicating in English, you will learn organically through necessity by speaking about things that you feel passionate about.
