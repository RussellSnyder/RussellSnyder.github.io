Highly Individualize Lessons Less boring stuff

Traditional language learning books and methods often teach grammar directly through charts and handouts. However, my teaching method focuses on teaching grammar through sitatuations that are relevant to you. The overall learning process in turn can be more efficient and longer lasting when lesson topics are agreed upon by the learner.

If you like coffee, we'll learn tenses by talking about coffee. If you like politics and comic books, we'll learn about hypotheticals by talking about Super Obama! It won't feel like a grammar lesson, but you will learn complex structures without trying. To learn more about individual taylored lessons and my teaching philosophy, check out the methodspage

Learn what you need or want at your own pace

There are many ways to learn a new language and each learner has unique needs. I strive to find the right method for each person.

Whether you are a quick learner or slow, you will learn using a blend of time tested methods tailored around your schedule and learning style. To learn more about my teaching methodology, check out the methods page

Fun learning is effective learning motivation is key

Fun and humor not only makes learning more enjoyable, but it actually allows connections made in your brain to be stronger! If you are not enjoying what you are studying, then you probably will not retain as much of it afterwards. This is the cornerstone of the methods I use for teaching. enjoyment is crucial in the learning process.
